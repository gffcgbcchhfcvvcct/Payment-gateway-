NexaPay is a unified mobile money payment gateway built by Elicate Technologies Limited, a fintech company headquartered in Ndola, Zambia. We give developers and merchants a single, powerful API to accept digital payments across Zambia, Zimbabwe, Botswana, and Namibia — without the complexity of integrating each mobile network separately.

01
Our Story

NexaPay was born out of a simple frustration: building a business in Southern Africa meant navigating a maze of separate mobile money APIs, incomplete documentation, unpredictable uptime, and no unified way to collect payments across borders. Every developer integrating payments in this region faced the same challenges — and every merchant felt the cost of that complexity.

In 2022, the founding team at Elicate Technologies Limited set out to change that. Based out of Ndola, Zambia — a city at the heart of the Copperbelt — we began building what we wished had existed: a clean, reliable, developer-first payment gateway designed specifically for Southern Africa.

We spent our first year deep in integrations — direct API partnerships with MTN Zambia, Airtel Money, EcoCash Zimbabwe, Orange Botswana, and MTC Namibia. We built redundancy, failover routing, real-time monitoring, and a merchant dashboard that made sense. We stress-tested everything. Then we opened to merchants.

Today NexaPay processes over 1 million transactions per year across four countries, supports 1,000+ active merchants, and maintains a 99.9% uptime guarantee. We are just getting started.

02
Our Mission

Our mission is to remove the friction from digital payments in Southern Africa — giving every developer, merchant, and business the tools to accept mobile money as easily as they would swipe a card anywhere else in the world.

We believe that access to reliable, affordable, and developer-friendly payment infrastructure is a catalyst for economic growth. When a small business in Lusaka can add a payment button to their website in an afternoon, when a marketplace in Harare can collect from customers across the region, when a SaaS startup in Gaborone can charge subscriptions in local currency — the entire ecosystem wins.

Payments should be infrastructure, not a barrier. That is the principle behind every design decision we make at NexaPay.

We achieve this mission by maintaining direct integrations with all major mobile money networks in our supported countries, providing best-in-class documentation, keeping our API simple, and pricing fairly — with no hidden fees and automatic volume discounts.

03
Our Vision

Our vision is to be the payment layer that powers the digital economy of Sub-Saharan Africa. We see a future where any business on the continent — regardless of size — can access the same quality of payment infrastructure available to Fortune 500 companies, at a fraction of the cost.

We are building toward a Southern Africa where:

  • Every mobile device is a point of sale and a payment terminal
  • Cross-border payments between SADC countries are instant and cheap
  • Small businesses can compete globally because payment collection is never the bottleneck
  • Developers in Africa build world-class products without fighting fragmented APIs
  • Financial inclusion reaches the last mile — rural merchants, informal traders, gig workers

NexaPay is currently live in four countries. Our roadmap includes expansion to South Africa, Malawi, Tanzania, and Mozambique by 2027, and a full Pan-African coverage by 2030.

08
Technology & Infrastructure

NexaPay is built on a modern, cloud-native infrastructure with African data centres at its core. Our platform is designed for high availability, low latency, and rapid network partner integrations.

Core Platform

  • RESTful JSON API with OpenAPI 3.0 specification — machine-readable and client-generatable
  • TLS 1.3 enforced on all endpoints; HSTS with one-year max-age
  • African data centres — data processed and stored in-country for each jurisdiction
  • Multi-region active-active deployment with automatic failover
  • Real-time webhook delivery with HMAC-SHA256 signing and 5x exponential retry

Network Integrations

  • Direct API partnerships (not aggregator-dependent) with all 12+ supported networks
  • Smart routing — automatically falls back to alternate network if primary is degraded
  • SIM swap detection via real-time telco APIs on MTN, Airtel, and EcoCash
  • Network health monitoring with sub-60-second alerting on degradation

Developer Experience

  • Open-source SDKs: JavaScript/TypeScript, Python, PHP (MIT licensed)
  • Full-featured sandbox mirroring production exactly
  • Interactive API reference with live "Try it" requests
  • Webhook replay and event inspector in the merchant dashboard

Our API is versioned with a stable v1 path. We guarantee backwards compatibility within major versions and provide a minimum 12-month deprecation notice for any breaking changes.
