How It Works

Get started accepting payments in four simple steps.

Step 01

Create an Account

Sign up as a merchant using your email or Google account. Provide your business details for verification across Zambia, Zimbabwe, Botswana, or Namibia.

Step 02

Get Verified

Our team reviews and verifies your merchant account. Once approved, you can generate your API keys and configure your preferred payment networks.

Step 03

Integrate the API

Use our comprehensive documentation to integrate the payment API into your website, mobile app, or platform. Our SDKs make it fast and straightforward.
